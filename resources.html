Resources

The Basics

Learn about the Hike Safe Card and why its important.

Absolutely pack The 10 Essential if not more! What if you had to survive a night.

Follow the Leave No Trace Guidelines.

Study the maps, select the appropriate trail for you. Some are more difficult than others. Purchase the AMC Mountain Guide, it comes with all the maps you need.

Where appropriate shoes, get fitted by a professional. The terrain can be tough on your feet.

Consider trekking poles. They can save your knees, help with water crossings and assist with balance.

If you are new to hiking, consider starting with the 52WAV It's where I started my journey.

For the love of god, don't wear cotton!
